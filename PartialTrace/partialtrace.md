
## Partial Trace: Definiton 

Let $V$ and $W$ be hilbert spaces and let $L(V\otimes W)$ and $L(V)$ be the vector space of lienar operators on $V \otimes W$ and $V$ respectively. Then we define the partial trace as an operator  $Tr_w: L\left(V \otimes W\right) \mapsto L(V)$ such that the following invariant holds.

$$\forall R \in V, \forall S \in W\colon  Tr_W (R \otimes S) = Tr (S)R$$

## Theorems/Properties

**Thoerem 1:**

$${\displaystyle \operatorname {Tr} _{W}(I_{V\otimes W})=\dim W\ I_{V}}$$

**Theorem 2:**

$$Tr _{W}(T(I_{V}\otimes S))=\operatorname {Tr} _{W}((I_{V}\otimes S)T)\quad \forall S\in \operatorname {L} (W)\quad \forall T\in \operatorname {L} (V\otimes W)$$
